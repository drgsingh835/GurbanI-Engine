# Punjabi Guftar: The Academic Punjabi Workspace

Welcome to the **Innovation Lab** for the Punjabi Guftar YouTube channel. This workspace is dedicated to preserving and promoting the "Prestige" register of the Punjabi language through high-fidelity digital content.

## 📂 Structure
- **01_Scripts_and_Research**: The scholarly core. Etymology and Gurmukhi scripting.
- **02_Visual_Assets**: Premium visual design, thumbnails, and AI calligraphy.
- **03_Audio_Laboratory**: Voiceovers and sound engineering.
- **04_Video_Production**: Automation scripts (FFmpeg) and video assembly.
- **05_Final_Deliverables**: The production-ready archive.
- **06_Governance**: Standards, SEO templates, and performance tracking.

## 🎯 Channel Objective
To elevate Punjabi from a "dialect" perception to its rightful place as a language of high literature, science, and formal discourse.
