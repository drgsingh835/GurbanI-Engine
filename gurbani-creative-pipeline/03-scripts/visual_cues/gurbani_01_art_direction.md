# ART DIRECTION: Pain is the Medicine

## Palette
- Deep Cosmic Indigo: #0B0E14
- Ethereal Gold: #D4AF37
- Dawn Crimson: #8B1E0F
- Muted Mist Gray: #7E8C99

## Visual Mood
An awe-inspiring journey from dark, anxious isolation into the vast, luminous peace of the cosmic order.

## Lighting & Textures
- Lighting: Volumetric, dusty rays of warm golden light cutting through thick, dark atmosphere; high-contrast chiaroscuro.
- Textures: Warm, cinematic 35mm film grain with floating dust motes and organic rough stone textures.

## Composition & Camera
- Composition: Centered symmetry to represent divine order; extreme wide shots representing scale; deep negative space.
- Framing: Slow, continuous camera movements (slow zooms and dollies), avoiding hand-held jitter.

## Style References
- The scale and volumetric lighting of Denis Villeneuve's *Dune*.
- The high-contrast nature photography of Ansel Adams.
- The meditative, natural color palette of Terrence Malick's *The Tree of Life*.

## DO NOT
- Do not use modern technology or electronics.
- Do not use neon, saturated pinks, or blues.
- Do not use fast camera movements, pans, or shaky-cam.
- Do not use flat, bright commercial studio lighting.
