# Academic Punjabi Style Guide

## 1. Linguistic Registers
- **Priority**: Formal/Academic Punjabi (Taksali/Standard).
- **Avoid**: Casual slang, excessive Hinglish/Urdu-isms unless historically relevant to the root.
- **Nuance**: Favor terms with documented Persian/Sanskrit/Arabic etymology that have a rich literary history in Punjabi.

## 2. Orthography (Gurmukhi Protocol)
Every video must feature:
- **Gurmukhi (ਗੁਰਮੁਖੀ)**: The primary script for all linguistic and academic content.
- **Transliteration**: Standardized IPA or Romanized Punjabi for international scholars.

## 3. Visual Aesthetic
- **Color Palette**: Dark charcoal, deep gold, and parchment cream.
- **Typography**: Clean, modern, and elegant Gurmukhi fonts.
- **Imagery**: Historical manuscripts, minimalist geometry, and high-quality calligraphy.

## 4. Metadata & SEO
- **Keywords**: `Punjabi Etymology`, `Academic Punjabi`, `Linguistic History`, `Gurmukhi Script`.
- **Tone**: Scholarly, respectful, and inviting.
