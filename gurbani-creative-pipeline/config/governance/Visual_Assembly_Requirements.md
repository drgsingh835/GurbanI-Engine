# Requirements: Visual Assembly Workflow

Since you will be providing the voiceovers (VO), here is the protocol for how I will "put visuals on it" to create the final video.

## 📥 1. What You Need to Provide
To ensure the visuals sync perfectly with your narration, please provide:

1.  **The Audio File**: Uploaded to `F:\Punjabi_Guftar_Workspace\04_Audio_Recording_Studio\01_Raw_VO`. (Preferably `.wav` or `.mp3`).
2.  **The Final Script**: Even if it's the one we drafted, confirm the final version so I can match the "Word Cards" (Gurmukhi) to your exact speech.
3.  **Key "Visual Hooks"**: Mention if there is a specific manuscript or image you want to see at a certain point (e.g., "At 00:45, show the root word etymology").

---

## 🛠️ 2. My Assembly Role (The Automation Logic)
Once I have your audio, I will perform the following steps:

1.  **Transcription & Sync**: I will analyze the audio to find the exact timestamps of key words.
2.  **Asset Generation**:
    *   **Word Cards**: Generating high-fidelity Gurmukhi typography for key terms.
    *   **B-Roll**: Generating 3-5 AI images (calligraphy, historical textures) based on the script's theme.
3.  **Visual Overlay (via FFmpeg)**:
    *   **Background**: Looping texture or B-roll.
    *   **Lower Thirds**: Displaying the word in Gurmukhi + Romanized transliteration.
    *   **Transitions**: Smooth fades between visual sections.
4.  **Rendering**: I will export a high-quality (1080p/4K) file ready for YouTube.
